About Threads of Africa

Our Story

Founded in Johannesburg in 2015 by visionary designer Lwazi Dlamini, Threads of Africa transforms streetwear into wearable art. Each piece tells a story of African heritage and modern urban aesthetics.

Mission

Craft streetwear that is visually striking and culturally meaningful, connecting wearers to South Africa's rich heritage while celebrating contemporary design.

Vision

To establish Threads of Africa as Africa's premier high-fashion streetwear label, recognized for creativity, precision, and cultural storytelling.
